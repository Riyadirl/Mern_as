Front-end web development focuses on what users see on the web browser screen. It is the “client-side” of web development.

Back-end development focuses on the behind the scenes of all websites. It is what makes your site interactive. Back-end development is also the “server-side” of web development.0

Front-end development task are:
  > User interface
  > creating web page with different types of layout.

Back-end development task are:  
  > Servers management
  > database management
  > Connecting to browser.
